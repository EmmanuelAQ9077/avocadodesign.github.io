Draw Your Name in Five Fonts

This project was quite hard, We had to draw out our names in 5 fonts, including our last name. We did this procedure with 5 fonts, Helvetica which is a Sans-Serif, Rockwell which is a Slab-Serif, Bodoni which is a Modern, Baskerville which is a Transitional and Garamond which is a Old Style. I struggled alot with drawing in Modern as most of the serif parts were very thin especially on the lowercase a and c. I ended up getting a score of 96%, I didn't expect to get such a high score for what felt like low effort.
