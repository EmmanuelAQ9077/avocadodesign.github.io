Branded Brochure

For this project, we were asked to make up a company, the logo, and make a brochure based off of it. I wanted to do an Airline that operated in Southeast Asia, I had the brilliant idea of naming it SouthEastern Air (Not to be confused with Southwest Air). Once I chose the name I then had to create a logo for it, I decided to incorporate the tail fin of a plane into my logo. For the colors of my designs, I used the colors of the flags of Vietnam, Cambodia, and the Philippines as an inspiration because this airline is supposed to offer somewhat cheap flights in Southeast Asia, it made sense to use the colors of these flags. Surprisingly, I made 15 + 5 decent designs using the same typeface, Berthold Akzidenz-Grotesk. I know it's shocking especially because I too didn't think I wouldn't need to switch over to a different typeface like Gotham or Frutiger for my designs.

Once I was done tweaking my logo it was now time to make the brochure.

For the brochure I gathered various images from the web, and I used a website to make the illustrations of the plane with my logos and colors plastered on to it. Honestly, I was quite satisfied with how the whole thing came out, I showed this to my iMentor and he was impressed with my quality of work.
